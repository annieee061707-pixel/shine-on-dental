About Our Practice

Where Clinical Excellence
Meets Compassionate Care

Shine On Multispeciality Dental Care has been a trusted name in Kammanahalli, Bengaluru since 2005. Led by senior professors and specialists from M.R. Ambedkar Dental College & Hospital, our clinic brings academic rigour and evidence-based treatment to every patient interaction.

We are committed to delivering painless, hygienic, and affordable care using modern equipment and proven clinical protocols. Whether it's a routine check-up for your child or advanced orthodontic work, every patient receives the same standard of specialist attention.
